WaxLimited

Get it on Vinyl.

WaxLimited is for artists and fans. We are working to give you a simple way to get your favorite music pressed and in your collection.


How it works.

We're like teespring but for records. We start with a great recording and a community of supporters. Once the pre-order goal is met, the artist gives approval and we make sure the vinyl is manufactured and delivered.

With your help, it's genuinely painless for the artists.

Why?

Because we want to use it, too.

WaxLimited is the result of our shared desire to get more music on wax. Your collection is a part of you. Now you can be a part of your collection.

Who we are.

We are Kyle Billings and Julian Weisser—two New England musicians with dismal musical compatibility (read: we cover a lot of musical ground) and a desire to change the way people interact with the world. Reach out to us. We want to know how we can make WaxLimited work for you.
