Get it on Vinyl.

WaxLimited helps artists press vinyl for their biggest fans. There are zero upfront costs, we handle production and shipping, and you earn money.


How it works.

It all starts with your music. Once enough fans pre-order through our site, we manufacture the records, mail them to your fans, and send you the profit.

It's genuinely painless, and the shipping is free.

Why?

Your music on vinyl is more than sound, it's a physical connection to your fans. We're here to help you press records for your most passionate supporters.

And because we want to use it, too.

Who we are.

We are Kyle Billings and Julian Weisser—two New England musicians with dismal musical compatibility (read: we cover a lot of musical ground) and a desire to change the way people interact with the world. Reach out to us. We want to know how we can make WaxLimited work for you.
